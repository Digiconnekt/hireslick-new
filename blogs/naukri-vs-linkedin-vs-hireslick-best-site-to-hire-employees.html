Naukri vs Linkedin vs Hireslick: Best Site to Hire Employees

The contemporary job market is dynamic. This dynamism has a certain effect on the hiring process. Today, several online services claim to help the hiring process or assist the companies for the same. However, the efficacy of those sites is in question. Employers find it difficult to choose the best site to hire employees especially to find remote workers.

Naukri, LinkedIn, and Hireslick; How to Find Your Perfect Hiring Partner?

Hiring sites have been constantly subjected to innovations in order to meet the expectations of the companies. They aim to bring out the best features to find the right candidates for the roles. Each of them uses distinct features to guide employers in the hiring process.

  • Naukri.com :
    Naukri.com stands as one of the oldest most trusted and best sites to hire employees. Naukri has long been the go-to place for employers to find remote workers in the Indian market. Its robust features, such as advanced search engines and applicant tracking systems eclipse roles in large companies. However sub-dated projects and the pricing structure is considered less flexible compared to other alternatives.
  • Linkedin :
    LinkedIn has changed how people find jobs and connect professionally. With more than 800 million users worldwide, many businesses could reach a wide range of people. Its special features like LinkedIn Recruiter help companies find and talk to the best people for the job. LinkedIn also helps people showcase their skills and build professional relationships, which is great for businesses looking to hire. However, some of its features cost a lot of money, which may not be feasible for small businesses.
